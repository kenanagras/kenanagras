🏛️ Areas of Expertise & Projects


🦌 Üçağızlı Caves (I & II)

By examining the morphology of the fallow deer from the Middle Paleolithic to the Upper Paleolithic periods, I demonstrate the impact of climatic and cultural changes on the fauna using modern morphometric approaches. This study provides a new layer of data for Paleolithic chronology in the Levant region.

🌍 GAYA Project (Gaziantep Pleistocene Survey)

While mapping Pleistocene-era settlements in the Gaziantep region, I manage the spatial distribution of the data using QGIS and statistical models. I combine the challenges of the fieldwork with the rigor of the data.

📊 Statistical Archaeology

I develop R- and Python-based analysis scripts to promote the use of quantitative methods in archaeology.
